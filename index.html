Ho Chi Minh City — District 1 (Hồ Chí Minh City, Vietnam)

Vibrant, historic, and modern — District 1 is the heart of Saigon, where colonial architecture, bustling markets, riverfront skyscrapers, and lively street food scenes meet.

Overview Top Attraction Image More Infomation

Why I love District 1?

District 1 is a compact area in central Ho Chi Minh City (also commonly called Saigon) where modern life and history sit side-by-side. Visitors can wander tree-lined boulevards past French colonial buildings, sample street-side phở and bánh mì, shop at the famous Ben Thanh Market, and watch the sunset from the Bitexco Financial Tower. District 1 is my favorite because it packs so many experiences within walking distance: museums, cafés, nightlife, and landmarks that give a snapshot of modern Vietnam.

More to Do!

Walk along Nguyễn Huệ walking street, catch a performance at the Saigon Opera House, or take a short river ferry ride. District 1's compact layout makes it easy to mix shopping, dining, and sightseeing in a single day.
